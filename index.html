Our Story

Born from a Love of Baking

Philip didn't set out to build a cookie brand — he set out to make the best cookie he'd ever tasted. What started as weekend experiments in a home kitchen quickly turned into something friends, family, and neighbors couldn't stop talking about.

Every week, Philip carefully selects a lineup of flavors, sources premium ingredients, and bakes every single cookie by hand. No assembly lines. No shortcuts. Just obsessive attention to texture, flavor, and that unmistakable warmth you can only get from something truly homemade.

Covet Cookies is built around one belief: that a great cookie isn't just a snack — it's a moment. A reason to pause, to share, to treat yourself without apology.

🧁 Small-batch, handcrafted every week — never frozen, never mass-produced
✨ Premium ingredients: real butter, pure vanilla, quality chocolate
